Honi Soit Wom*n's - Issue S1#11

Collection of film stills from a dream girl's day.

The Wom*n's Honi issueÂ curated some of the most insightful and interesting articles of the semester, presenting the "female narrative" as a striking balance of presence, strength, awareness and disillusion.

Amongst being one of the most interesting issues of the semester, there was a controversy surrounding the use of the cover image showing an Israeli suicide bomber. The response was an enquiry into the practise of free speech and a combative stance on what was ethically liberal. An odd mix it is; the admiration of beauty within the context of civil strife...

"a day in the life of your dream girl" is a series of film stills presenting the vapid, insightful and loving experiences felt by the hypothetical "dream girl". Absurdist, allusory to "manic pixie dream girl" tropes and drenched in a satirical stance against artificial mass culture, the series attempts to regain power in the "real girl".
