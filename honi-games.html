Honi Soit Games - Issue 90

Series of line drawings and tracings of faces and people.

This Honi issueÂ revolved around the evolution and zeitgeist of characters in the Game Era. The inspiration for the work came from the subject matter of form and body in games and the interesting concepts of free-thinking and autonomy associated with the concept of humanoid, the uncanny.

These sketches were created with black ink on paper and inspired by the beauty in human faces and features. From Roman-esque noses to wonderful eyes and lips, I wanted to present beauty in organic hues and silky shapes.
