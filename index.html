Sooo how did this start?

About 3 years ago, I was writing a screenplay called Tsar, about high school kids living in an exclusive gated LA neighbourhood. The story centred around the main character, Tsar. Angel hair, beautiful, kind, golden boy type who wore vintage t-shirts from his dad’s closet. Because his dad, back in his heyday, used to smoke blunts and drive to the beach at midnight. It was an ode to all things I loved in the culture of a world so far removed from my own suburban Australian upbringing. Since then, the story has evolved. I am no longer a teen, my thoughts have changed, my style and sense of belonging too. Tsar has evolved with this. It will forever be my baby and a relic and reflection of my obsession with beauty, culture and the world around. So, the only thing I’m saying is: Join the club x
